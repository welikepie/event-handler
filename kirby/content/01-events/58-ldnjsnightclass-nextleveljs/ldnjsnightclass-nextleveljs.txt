Title: London JS Night Class: Next Level JavaScript
----
Text: 
On the 12th of February, (link: http://www.twitter.com/benhowdle text: Ben Howdle) will be hosting a workshop in which you can learn to take your JavaScript to the next level.

(image: nextlevel.png class: full-width)

### Who is it for?

Comfortable with jQuery but want to go further? Want to be able to build entire applications using JavaScript? Struggling to organise and architect your code? After this evening workshop, you'll have taken your JavaScript to next level! 

### Who is it taught by?

This workshop is being run by (link: http://www.twitter.com/benhowdle text: Ben Howdle. Ben is a software developer for Kashflow and describes himself as a "JavaScript guy at heart". Ben has worked on many projects over the years including (link: http://benhowdle.im/svgeezy/ text: SVGeezy), (link: http://benhowdle.im/cortado/ text: Cortado) and (link: http://heisenbergjs.github.io/heisenberg/ text: Heisenberg.js); whilst also being one half of the (link: http://upfrontpodcast.com/ text: Upfront Podcast) on Front-End Web Development. He also writes a (link: http://benhowdle.im/archive/ text: blog) that covers many things including JS.

### What you'll learn

* Memory Profiling
* Performance
* Coding Conventions
* How to maintain your projects

### Requirements

* Must be familiar with JavaScript
* Please bring your laptop with your text editor of choice installed
