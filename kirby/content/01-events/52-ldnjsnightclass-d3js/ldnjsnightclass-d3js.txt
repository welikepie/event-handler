Title: London JS Night Class: D3.js

Text: 
On the 21st of January, (link: http://www.twitter.com/timruffles text: Tim Ruffles) will be hosting an evening workshop in which you can learn to use D3.js

(image: d3js.png class: full-width)

### Who is it for?

This class will give you a firm grasp of the core of D3 and you'll leave knowing enough about D3 to start on any visualisation project you fancy.

### Who is it taught by?

This workshop is being run by (link: http://www.twitter.com/timruffles text: Tim Ruffles). Tim is a Ruby & JavaScript Developer living and working in London. Having previously worked for Skimlinks as their tech lead, he now spends time mentoring others and working on (link: http://www.sidekickjs.com text: SidekickJS), his code-quality tracking tool. You can find him speaking about the web at (link: http://www.lanyrd.com/timruffles text: various events). 

### What you'll learn

* D3 philosophy - why is it this way?
* D3 DOM API
* Selections
* Contexts: update, enter, exit
* Object constancy
* Animations
* Hierarchy
* Scales
* Layouts

### Requirements

* Familiarity with JavaScript
* Please bring your laptop with your text editor of choice installed
