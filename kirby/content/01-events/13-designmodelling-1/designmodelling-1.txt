Title: Design Modelling Workshop
----
Text: 

Join (link: https://twitter.com/jude_pullen text: Jude Pullen), award winning designer and engineer at Dyson UK on a full day design modelling workshop. Working from sketches; Jude will teach you how to make physical prototypes out of card, foam and plastic. Learn how to make joints & curves, folds & cuts and how to put it all together.

What you'll learn:

* How to turn your ideas into a final prototype
* Techniques to handle cardboard, foam and plastic
* The score and snap technique for modelling with acrylic
* How to make curves, joints, folds and cuts. 
* How to use a glue gun to piece your work together

If you have a particular idea of what you'd like to prototype at the workshop, then you can email us at (email: contact@eventhandler.co.uk) and let us know!
