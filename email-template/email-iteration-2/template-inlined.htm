London JS School Trip

 

Hello, London JS School Trip attendees, we hope this email reaches you in good health! We just thought we'd remind you that this Saturday (30th June, 2012) is the day in which the event will take place.

If you can't remember where it is, you can find it at the Central Foundation Boys School in London.

     Central Foundation Boys School,
     Cowper Street, London,
     EC2A 4SH

For those of you unfamiliar with the area, the nearest tube station is Old Street Underground Station on the Northern Line. If travelling by bus, the closest bus stop is Old Street Roundabout which is served by the 205, 214, 43 and 135. Bear in mind it is a Saturday in central London (extremely busy), so make sure to leave with enough time!

Registration starts at 9:30AM at the venue and the first talks for both the A Track and Blackberry Track start at 10AM.

Although not compulsory, to get the full experience on the day we advise you to bring a laptop so you can take part in the workshops.

We look forward to seeing you.
