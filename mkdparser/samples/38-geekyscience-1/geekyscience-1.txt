Text: 

We're excited to announce a brand new monthly event, GeekyScience!

(image: geekyscience.png class: full-width)

GeekyScience is an exciting evening of learning and entertainment. Each month, experts in their field will come together to share what they know, related to an overarching scientific theme that will change each month. GeekyScience is both curated and hosted by (link: http://www.twitter.com/helenarney text: Helen Arney), award-winning comedian and physics graduate. If you want to learn about the things that live deep within the ocean, how to make an asteroid or what an explosion scientist gets up to day-to-day; GeekyScience is something you don’t want to miss. 

## Septembers' Theme: Evolution

From Mendel's discoveries on inheritance to Darwin's theory of natural selection, the theory of evolution has led to an immense amount of scientific research and discovery. (link: http://www.twitter.com/harpistkat text: Dr. Kat Arney), Science Information Manager at Cancer Research UK, will be joining us to share her insights into the evolution of Cancer and (link: http://www.twitter.com/adamrutherford text: Dr. Adam Rutherford), journalist and presenter, will be using hip hop in his exploration of evolution and synthetic biology.
